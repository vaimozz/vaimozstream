# VaimozStream Branding Guidelines

## Brand Overview

**VaimozStream** adalah platform live streaming berbasis web yang powerful dan mudah digunakan untuk content creators yang ingin streaming ke multiple platform secara bersamaan dengan infrastruktur mandiri.

## Brand Name & Tagline

- **Primary Name**: VaimozStream
- **Tagline**: Web-Based Multi-Platform Streaming
- **Description**: Cloud streaming solution dengan FFmpeg
- **Brand Mascot/Logo**: Dual-fish swirl (representing Lamongan's iconic **Bandeng** & **Lele** mascots swimming dynamically in a cyan-to-blue gradient around a central play button).

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Cyan**: `#06B6D4` - Main accent and energetic brand color
- **Primary Blue**: `#2563EB` - Secondary brand color for gradients and hovers
- **Primary Gradient**: Linear gradient from `#06B6D4` to `#2563EB` (Cyan-to-Blue)

### Neutral Colors (Premium Dark Mode)
- **Deep Background**: `#121212` - Base dark background
- **Surface Card**: `#252525` - Card backgrounds (Dark 800)
- **Text Primary**: `#FFFFFF` - Primary body text
- **Text Secondary**: `#AFAFAF` - Secondary description text

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary**: Inter, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif
- **Monospace**: 'Monaco', 'Menlo', 'Ubuntu Mono', monospace (for logs and codes)

## Logo & Visual Elements

### Logo Files
- `logo.svg` - Primary logo (circular dual-fish swirling play button)
- `logo_mobile.svg` - Mobile version (optimized smaller resolution)
- **Concept**: The logo blends **local wisdom** (incorporating Lamongan's iconic fish mascots) with **high-tech functionality** (dynamic swirl forming a circle around the play button, symbolizing continuous streaming).

## UI Component Guidelines

### Buttons & Inputs
- **Primary**: Gradasi Cyan-to-Blue (Cyan-to-Blue gradient)
- **Glassmorphism**: Backdrop blur with high opacity borders to feel premium and state of the art.

## Terminology
- Use **VaimozStream** (not "Vaimoz Stream", "vaimozstream", or "vaimoz-stream" in customer-facing titles).
- Use **streaming** (lowercase) in general context.
- Use **rotations** for rotation streaming items.
